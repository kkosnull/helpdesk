17942/P1534 - Camshaft Timing Adj. Bank2 (N208): Short to Plus

Possible Symptoms

  • MIL On
  • Poor engine performance

Possible Causes

  • Wiring harness issue
  • Failed N208 adjuster (electrical)
  • Failed ECM

Possible Solutions

  • Ohm test N208 adjuster, see repair manual for specifications.
  • Inspect wiring harness to and from N208 adjuster.
